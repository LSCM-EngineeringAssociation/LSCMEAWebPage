About Us

The Engineering Association consists of eager students pursuing engineering, computer science, and related fields. We love to deconstruct things to learn about them. Our planned semester projects include: building a website from scratch that is hosted on our own server and building a robotic arm that can copy the user's hand movements. This is a club for those who enjoy learning. Even if you're clueless about programming or engineering, you are welcome to join!

When We Meet

We meet every Monday and Wednesday at 3:30 - 4:30pm in room B108. Come join the engineering fun!
