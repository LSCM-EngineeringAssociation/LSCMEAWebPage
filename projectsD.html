Self-Driving Car

Spring 2023

We disassembled a small remote controlled car to learn about the structure and functions of the parts and wires. We used the donkeycar software and built our own self-driving car that supported a raspberry pi, 3D printed frame, a camera, and more! With the donkeycar and color recognition software, the car was capable of driving around a small, preset track.
